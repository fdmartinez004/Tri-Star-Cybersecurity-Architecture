### Cybersecurity Roadmap: Tri-Star Alignment
- **Objective**: Transition from Star 1 (Manual/Reactive) to Star 3 (Automated/Strategic).
- **Core Alignment**: 
  - NIST CSF: Primary focus on the **Govern** and **Identify** functions.
  - Texas DIR: Aligns with the **Texas Cybersecurity Framework (TCF)** reporting requirements.
